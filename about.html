About

Phiev CompuTech

What we do

Phiev CompuTech gives you a chance to quickly and easily find any electrical gadget you want and have it delivered to your home in no time, regardless of your location, as long as it is in Africa. We also provide consultation services on all computer related topics.

Why Customers Love Us

We have been in the business for quite a while now, and in that time we have not only managed to make close relationships with numerous suppliers all over the world, but also to recognize what people need. This means that we are always able to offer all the latest electrical gadgets, great prices, reliable service, fast delivery and premium customer support.

Our Story

Phiev CompuTech

Beginning

Phiev CompuTech website was launched in 2021, but its story actually began a few years before that when two brothers and the young brother's girlfriend decided to go into business together. We started selling phones on social media platforms, but our combined ambition, drive and abilities soon made us look for new challenges and new markets. Starting an online shop provided for and allowed us to develop a strong international presence in a number of African countries.

Today

Collective experience of our team members and the years we have spent in the business allowed us to develop a vast network of suppliers, ensuring that our customers will always find what they are looking for.

Our affordability, fast and reliable delivery, and the fact that you will always find what you are looking for in our offer, have made us stand out in the market, but they are simply symptoms of our dedication to what we are doing and our desire to constantly keep improving. We know that in order to do that, we need to keep in close touch with our customers and listen to their suggestions and critiques. This is why our customer service, is always there to answer any question that you may have.

Our Policies

Phiev CompuTech

Returns & Refunds

PHIEV's core mantra is “Customer Satisfaction” for this we give a hassle-free Refund policy to all our customers.

The process to be followed for Return Policy:

~ Phiev reserves the right to reject the returns for any product depending on the damage of the product.
~ Notify us through the mail before 24hrs from the day the product received.
~ The customer will be notified through mail or message about the return acceptance within two days from the day of refund application.
~ At the time of product return the product should have all the accessories and should be in the original packaging.
~ At the time of the return process make sure that you submit the invoice as proof of purchase and ask for acknowledgment copy.
~ The product return acceptance will be notified through mail.
~ If there is a manufacturing issue with the product the time of return will depend on the commitment given by the supplier. (normally 7 working days).

Warranty & After Purchase Services

PHIEV's products mainly have the seller warranty.
~ Most products have 6 months of free warranty provided by Phiev Computech
~ In Africa, with an original invoice you are entitled to 6 months service at an authorized service centre. In a case of improper functioning of the product or malfunction, the product is sent directly to the authorized service centre.
~ In case that there is no service centre provided in the customer’s country, the customer needs to contact our Customer Support at warranty@phiev.co.zw, and our agent will provide you all the necessary shipping details to the closest service centre. Shipping costs to service centre (one way) will be covered by the customer. Please, keep in mind that if you don't send within the deadline set for you by Phiev support centre, you will lose the right to send the device to the service centre.
~ Phiev will determine the condition of the product and offer you one of the following options:
~ Service: you will be forwarded to the closest service centre (official MobileShop service or other).
~ Replacement: The same product that you ordered will be shipped to you.
~ Refund: if all else fails, you will be refunded excluding insurance costs.
